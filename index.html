Project Overview

The PulseGen project aims to develop a pulse motor that combines mechanical motion and electricity generation in a single device. By leveraging blockchain technology, we’re creating a transparent funding model through the PGGN token to support this innovative research.

Our goal is to create a prototype that demonstrates energy efficiency and scalability, with applications in renewable energy, transportation, and industrial systems.

Research Goals

Our research focuses on developing a pulse motor that:

  • Generates electricity while performing mechanical work
  • Maximizes energy efficiency through pulsed electromagnetic fields
  • Scales for applications in renewable energy and electric vehicles

Funds raised through PGGN will support prototype development, testing, and patent applications.
